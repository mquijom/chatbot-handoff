1. Present at least one (1) valid ID.

Note: UnionBank reserves the right to require customers to present more than one valid ID to positively establish identity, permanent address, and source of funds. Below is a list of accepted valid IDs, in order of the Bank's preference.

  1. Passport, including those issued by foreign governments
  2. Seaman's book
  3. Driver's license
  4. NBI clearance
  5. GSIS e-Card/SSS card
  6. License to own/Permit to carry firearm
  7. Professional Regulatory Commission (PRC) identification card/Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP) identification cards
  8. Tax identification card
  9. Alien Certificate of Registration (ACR)/Immigrant Certificate og Registration (ICR)/Special Investor Resident Visa (SIRV)/PRA Special Resident Visa (SRRV)
  10. OFW identification card
  11. Government office and GOCC identification cards (e.g., Armed Forces of the Philippines, Home Development Mutual Fund, etc.)
  12. OFW identification card
  13. Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PhilHealth) identification card
  14. Certification from the National Council for the Welfare of Disabled (NCWDO)
  15. Police clearance
  16. Digitized voter's identification card
  17. DSWD Certification
  18. Company identification cards issued by private entities or institutions registered or supervised or regulated either by the BSP, SEC or the Insurance Commission
  19. Senior citizen identification card
  20. Postal identification card
  21. Barangay certification

Notes:

  • Your ID/s should not be expired and must include your photo.
  • Your ID/s must be authentic and reliable. The Bank officers/personnel may require you to present other IDs to be satisfied as to your true identity or that of the principal opening the account.
  • Please present your original ID/s to the Bank. For companies that submit copies of the original IDs, the Corporate Secretary should attest to their authenticity through a notarized Secretary's Certificate.
  • The validity/expiry of an NBI clearance is 1 year from the date of issuance.
  • The ACR I-Card is for foreign residents only.

Also, please note the additional requirements for the following cases:

a. Sole Proprietorship

The photocopy of the Business Name Certificate of Affirmative Business Name Certification with the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) is acceptable as long as you present the original document to ensure that the copy is an authentic and true reproduction of the original.

b. Resident-Foreigners or Aliens

While the visa stamp by Bureau of Immigration (BI) indicating the alien's point-of-entry is not a regulatory mandate, the same is an indicia for purposes of due diligence in establishing the status of the resident-foreigner or alien in the Philippines, and in ensuring that the individual has entered the country legally. To be acceptable, please present your original Passport and ACR I-card (for permanent resident or tourist of more than 180 days) as identification documents.
